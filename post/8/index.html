Summary of Notation

13 Jul 2019

RL中的符号说明:

  1. 大写：随机变量
  2. 小写：随机变量的值或标量函数（返回值为标量）
  3. 小写粗体：实值向量或随机变量向量
  4. 大写粗体：矩阵

符号 描述
$v_{\pi}(s)$ value of state $s$ under policy $\pi$ (expected return) state-value
$v_{*}(s)$ value of state $s$ under the optimal policy $\pi$
$q_{\pi}(s,a)$ value of taking action $a$ in state $s$ under policy $\pi$ action-value
$q_{*}(s,a)$ value of taking action $a$ in state $s$ under the optimal policy $\pi$
$\pi$ policy (decision-making rule)
$\pi(a|s)$ probability of taking action $a$ in state $s$ under stochastic policy $\pi$
